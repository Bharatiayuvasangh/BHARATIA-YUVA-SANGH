About Us

Bharatia Yuva Sangh is a youth-led organization dedicated to addressing social issues and fostering community development. Inspired by Swami Vivekananda, we aim to create a sustainable and prosperous future for our village and beyond.

Our Initiatives

Cleanliness Drives

Organizing regular community-led cleanliness drives inspired by the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan.

Plantation Campaigns

Large-scale tree plantation drives to promote environmental sustainability in the community.

Dustbin Installation

Installing 500 dustbins in villages to improve waste management and cleanliness.
